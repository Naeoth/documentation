== Did you click the confirmation link in the email ?

When you created your account, we sent you an email with a confirmation link.
You must click the confirmation link in order to validate your account and be able to use ZetaPush services.
This confirmation link is valid for 7 days.

If you didn't click in time, your account has been deleted and you must create it again.
