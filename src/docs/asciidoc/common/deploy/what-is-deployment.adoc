In the _deployment_ with ZetaPush, you have 2 parts :

* Hosting and scalability of the back end
* Hosting of the front end

== Back end

When you deploy the back end part (_custom cloud services_) of your application, we take care of the hosting of your code. In addition, we manage the scability of your application.

== Front end

For the front end part, we hosting your application and return you an unique URL to access to your application. We also link your front end logic with the differents services you need (_custom cloud services_ or _cloud services_ provided by ZetaPush).
